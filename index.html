About UMANG

We present to you ,RKGIT's college fest.UMANG with its inception in the year 2009,is the most significant festival for RKGIT Ghaziabad. Spread across a span of two days,the fest provides students pursuing any course of all years and branch a chanbe to participate and explore.Be it an exposure into the different worlds of business and management,a chance to discuss world issues at an international model UN conference,participating and showcasing talent in a cultural extravaganza or showing their competitive spirit through sports,UMANG has it all.With immense and growing national and international participation,UMANG aims at being one of the premier fests of the country foran unforgettable experience for all.
