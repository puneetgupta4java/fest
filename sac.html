STUDENT ACTIVITIES COUNCIL

About SACUmang

"The SAC brainstorms all kinds of interesting and unique programs, events, and services that make the campus feel alive — with a mindful eye to diversity at all times."
College is about seeing things from a fresh perspective. Opening our eyes to more than just studies.Most of what we plan will change your life for the better in social, physical, intellectual or emotional ways.SAC ensures smooth and continuous conduct of student activities in the Institute. It also aims at quick and proper resolution of the issues of the students. SAC also provides an opportunity to the students to be engaged in the extracurricular activities apart from their regular curriculum, and present and enhance their creativity, technical and managerial skills.Students Activity Council (SAC) is a union of the students to address their concerns and for organization of extracurricular activities in the Institute. It is totally managed, organized and maintained by the students themselves. SAC contains 4 different Activity Councils (Cultural, Literary,Hobby and Sports).The Students Senate is the overall governing body of SAC; all the decision-making regarding the students is done here. Through SAC, students will develop leadership, program management, social responsibility, and interpersonal skills. Since our funds come from student money, SAC makes it a priority to organize events both appealing to students and diverse in nature.
